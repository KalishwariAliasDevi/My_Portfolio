Introduction

This is my Inventory Dashboard project built in Excel. The data included employee info, product categories, warehouse locations, price, and profit. I used Pivot Tables to summarize profit by state, category, year, and monthly order count, and then created an interactive dashboard using those insights.

Objective

    The primary goal of this project is to create a visual and interactive Excel dashboard that enables individuals to:

  • To track total products, profit, and profit %.
  • To identify top products and categories.
  • To analyze profit trends by year and month.
  • To filter inventory data by region, category, year, and status.

Result

  • Identified top profit-generating products and categories.
  • Tracked monthly order and profit trends.
  • Highlighted high and low performing regions.
  • Helped in making better stock and sales decisions.
