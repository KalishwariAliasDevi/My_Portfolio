Introduction

This project features a Sales Dashboard built using Excel. I used structured data containing date, product, unit price, quantity, region, and country, along with supporting sheets for customers and sales targets. I created Pivot Tables to analyze total sales, product trends, and regional performance, and turned them into an interactive dashboard.

Objective

    To visualize sales data across time, products, and regions for better business insights

  • To track overall and monthly sales performance.
  • To identify top-selling products and key customers.
  • To analyze sales by region and country using visuals.
  • To compare actual sales with monthly targets for better planning.

Result

    The Sales Dashboard provided a clear view of sales performance across products, time, and regions. It revealed:

  • Top-performing products and high-value customers.
  • Underperforming regions needing attention or new strategies.
  • Months where sales exceeded or fell short of targets.
  • Seasonal trends and patterns in product demand.
