Introduction

Personal financial management is essential for maintaining financial stability and making informed decisions. In this project, I developed a Finance Expenses Dashboard using Microsoft Excel to analyze income and expenditure patterns over time. The dataset included essential attributes such as account type, transaction date, amount, description, category, and sub-category. With this raw data, I organized, cleaned, and transformed it into meaningful insights through Pivot Tables and interactive visuals. This dashboard serves as a complete financial snapshot that helps track money flow, control overspending, and set achievable savings goals.

Objective

    The primary goal of this project is to create a visual and interactive Excel dashboard that enables individuals to:

  • Track total income and monthly expenses accurately.
  • Analyze spending by category and sub-category.
  • Compare net income vs. expenses on a monthly basis.
  • Identify major spending areas such as Rent, Groceries, Living, and Discretionary costs.
  • Monitor financial health through key indicators like Top Expense Categories, Savings Trends, and Monthly Cash Flow.

Result

    The Finance Expenses Dashboard provided a clear and structured view of financial activities. It revealed:

  • Opportunities to cut down discretionary expenses and increase savings.>

This dashboard has helped in setting realistic budgets, avoiding overspending, and planning for future financial goals with confidence.
