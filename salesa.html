Home / Sales Analysis Dashboard

Introduction

This project showcases an interactive Amazon Sales Dashboard created using Power BI. The dataset included fields like product category, description, price, reviews, shipment, and order date. I cleaned the data by removing duplicates, handling nulls, converting values to numeric, and extracting month/week from dates. Using this, I built a dashboard to analyze sales and reviews across time and categories.

Objective

    To visualize sales data across time, products, and regions for better business insights

  • To track total sales and product performance across time.
  • To analyze top products by sales and customer reviews.
  • To visualize trends using KPIs, charts, and filters.
  • To create an interactive dashboard using slicers for category and quarter.

Result

    The Sales Dashboard provided a clear view of sales performance across products, time, and regions. It revealed:

  • Men Shoes had the highest year-to-date (YTD) sales.
  • SanDisk products received the most customer reviews.
  • Clear insights on monthly and weekly sales/review trends.
  • Helped understand customer engagement and best-performing categories.
